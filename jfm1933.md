Esther Morton is the furthest that I can trace the given name "Esther". 1841 census notes birth place as England. The David Hutchesons of Inverkeithing were particularly confusing because there were three generations of David Hutcheson all living at the same time. After the death of Esther Hutcheson (n√©e Morton) David Hutcheson lived with his granddaughter also named Esther Hutcheson, which added to the confusion.
